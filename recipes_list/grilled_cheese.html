Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Butter
  • Mayonaisse

Instructions

Begin by taking two slices of bread and buttering one side of each. On one of the slices, on the side that is not buttered, apply mayonaise and sliced cheese. Press the sandwich together so that the buttered sides of both slices of bread are facing outward. Place a pan on your stovetop. Place the sandwich in the pan. Turn stove to medium-high. When bread is toasted golden on one side, flip sandwich and repeat. Once cheese is melted, remove from pan. Turn off stove. Enjoy!
